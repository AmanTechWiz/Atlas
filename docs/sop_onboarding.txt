ACME CORPORATION — EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
Document ID: SOP-ONB-014
Version: 3.0
Effective Date: 2026-02-01
Owner: People Operations
Audience: People Operations, Hiring Managers, IT, Facilities

================================================================================
1. PURPOSE
================================================================================

This SOP defines the standardized process for onboarding new employees at
ACME Corporation. The goal is to ensure every new hire is productive,
connected, and compliant with company policy by the end of their first
30 days.

================================================================================
2. SCOPE
================================================================================

Applies to all full-time, part-time, and contract employees of ACME
Corporation, regardless of location or department.

================================================================================
3. PRE-START (DAYS -7 TO -1)
================================================================================

3.1 OFFER AND BACKGROUND CHECK
- Hiring manager extends verbal offer
- People Ops issues written offer letter via DocuSign
- Background check and reference checks are initiated (target: complete
  within 5 business days)

3.2 IT AND ACCESS PROVISIONING
Once the offer is accepted, IT provisions:
- Corporate laptop (Windows 11 or macOS, per user preference)
- Active Directory account and SSO credentials
- Email address in the @acmecorp.example domain
- VPN access for remote work
- Default access to Slack, Google Workspace, and the company intranet

Target completion: 2 business days before start date.

3.3 WORKSPACE AND EQUIPMENT
Facilities prepares a desk, monitor, keyboard, and mouse. For fully
remote employees, a home-office equipment stipend of up to $1,000 USD
is available.

================================================================================
4. DAY 1
================================================================================

4.1 WELCOME SESSION (09:00 - 10:30)
A People Ops coordinator greets the new hire, reviews the day's agenda,
and walks them through the benefits enrollment, direct deposit, and
emergency contact forms.

4.2 IT SETUP AND ORIENTATION (10:30 - 12:00)
IT walks the new hire through laptop setup, VPN, password managers,
and the standard tool stack. Multi-factor authentication is mandatory
and must be configured on day 1.

4.3 TEAM INTRODUCTION LUNCH (12:00 - 13:30)
Hiring manager takes the new hire to lunch with their immediate team.

4.4 COMPLIANCE TRAINING ASSIGNMENT (13:30 - 14:30)
New hire is enrolled in the mandatory compliance courses on the
learning management system (LMS):
- Information Security Awareness (1 hour)
- Code of Conduct (45 minutes)
- Anti-Harassment Training (1 hour)
- Data Privacy and GDPR basics (1 hour)
All four must be completed within the first 14 days.

================================================================================
5. WEEK 1
================================================================================

5.1 ROLE-SPECIFIC TRAINING
Hiring manager runs role-specific training sessions covering tools,
processes, and key contacts.

5.2 BUDDY ASSIGNMENT
Each new hire is paired with a "buddy" — a peer outside their direct
reporting line — for informal questions and cultural onboarding.

5.3 1-ON-1S
Hiring manager holds a 30-minute 1:1 every day for the first week to
check in on questions, blockers, and overall experience.

================================================================================
6. DAYS 8 - 30
================================================================================

6.1 WEEKLY 1:1S
After week 1, the cadence moves to weekly 30-minute 1:1s with the
hiring manager.

6.2 FIRST PROJECT
By the end of week 2, the new hire should be assigned a first
discrete project with clear success criteria and a target completion
date within the 30-day window.

6.3 30-DAY CHECK-IN
A formal 30-day check-in is held with the new hire, their manager,
and a People Ops representative. Feedback is captured in the HRIS.

================================================================================
7. RESPONSIBILITIES
================================================================================

ROLE                | RESPONSIBILITY
--------------------|----------------------------------------------
Hiring Manager      | Owns the onboarding plan; runs 1:1s
People Operations   | Coordinates logistics, paperwork, training
IT                  | Provisions hardware and access on time
Facilities          | Prepares physical workspace
Buddy               | Provides informal peer support
New Hire            | Completes required training on time

================================================================================
8. ESCALATION
================================================================================

Any blocker in onboarding should be escalated to the People Operations
lead within 24 hours. Critical access issues (e.g., no laptop on day 1)
are escalated to the IT Director immediately.
